During the checkout process, customers can effortlessly choose a delivery date and time for their orders.

== Description ==
If you happen to be operating an e-commerce store on WooCommerce, then the **Order Delivery Date Time & Pickup for WooCommerce** plugin is undoubtedly an indispensable tool that you would not want to miss out on. 

**Order Delivery Date Time & Pickup for WooCommerce** plugin allows your customers to specify a specific date and time for delivery or pickup when they checkout their order. 

Additionally, it also provides them with a selection of pickup locations to choose from.

As the store administrator, you have full authority over the delivery or pickup schedule and can easily make modifications if necessary. Furthermore, the plugin is straightforward to set up and configure. Implementing this plugin can streamline your store's delivery and pickup process and significantly enhance customer satisfaction.

= How to add delivery date in WooCommerce? =
Navigate to Menu Bright Plugins > Order Delivery Settings and choose the Delivery Date Settings Tab. Toggle on the delivery date option and select the specific days you want to enable for delivery. Once you've enabled it, a new field will appear in the checkout form where customers can select the desired delivery date.
